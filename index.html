It’s Not Your Product. It’s What You Missed.

It’s 2027. You’re doing $800,000 months.
I just launched to $600 in sales and I’m humiliated.

What three things did you fix in the next launch that 10x’d it?
The positioning shift.
The new avatar.
The pain point you missed.

Save me before I quit.

How to Use This Prompt

  • Paste into ChatGPT or Claude
  • Add your niche (AI, info products, SaaS, services)
  • Force specifics, not encouragement

Expected Output

The fatal positioning mistake

The wrong avatar you were talking to

The pain point you underestimated

What changed in the next launch

Optional Add-On

Ask the AI to rewrite your offer in one sentence after answering.

Bonus: Get Brutal Honesty

Add this line at the end:
“Be ruthless. Assume my ego is the problem.”

Power Variations

Change the number

$800,000 → your aspirational target

Replace “launch”

Funnel · Offer · Course · SaaS

Add constraints

No ads · Same product · Same audience size

This turns coping into correction.
